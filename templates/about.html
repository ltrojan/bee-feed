Our association is one of the oldest local Beekeeping Associations in Northern Ireland. We are affilliated to the Ulster Beekeeping Association.

We are managed by an elected committee in accordance with our Constitution. Currently we have over 100 members and continue to grow.

The role of our Association is to serve our local community with support and education helping us to become better Beekeepers.

Activities include beekeeping, education and training courses, mentoring and advice, demonstrations and apiary meetings.

We have an informative, stimulating and entertaining programme of monthly events and presentations.

Meetings are held on the 4th Wednesday of each month in Andrews Memorial Primary School, Comber (click the pin for directions).

Throughout the summer months we also run a programme at our Apiary site.

Funds are raised through our membership and various other events held throughout the year.
