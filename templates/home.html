Welcome to the Killincy Beekeepers Association Webpage

{% endblock %} {% block text %}

We are an association of beekeepers based in the Killinchy District and surrounding area of County Down in Northern Ireland. Our aim is to support local beekeepers, organise the educational courses and social events.

Association meetings are held on the 4th Wednesday of each month, providing an informative and entertaining programme of talks and events throughout the year.

If you are not a beekeeper yet, we hope this site will help you become better informed about bees and beekeeping and be impressed and amazed (like the rest of us) by all that beekeeping entails.

Please feel free to contact us for any enquiries you may have.
